What is Gifify?

In short, Gifify is a pain-free Video-to-Gif converter. It accepts
a variety of formats and lets you customize it with either video
effects or text. Though a simple application, the learning experience I had
using Django was invaluable.

For this project, we were also required to follow the Waterfall Model.
